Yongze Song is a Lecturer and Doctorial Supervisor at Curtin University, Australia. He is a Fellow of Royal Geographical Society (with IBG), UK, and Editorial Board member of "GIScience & Remote Sensing" (IF 6.238, Q1). He is a Scientific Program Committee Member of the Association of Geographic Information Laboratories in Europe (AGILE), and lifetime member of International Association for Mathematical Geosciences (IAMG). He is recipient of 12 awards at international and national levels.

Yongze Song has published 38 peer-reviewed journal articles (ResearchGate). The articles include two ESI highly cited papers and 33 articles published on JCR Q1 journals, including IJGIS, RSE, IEEE T-GRS, RSER, IEEE T-ITS, IEEE T-GRSL, IEEE JSTARS, GRS, IJRS and EHJ. His h-index is 16 (Google Scholar). He developed 7 geospatial data analysis models and published 5 R open-source packages on CRAN for the innovative models with over 65,000 downloads globally.
